The Survey

Your experience deserves to be heard.

This survey is for medical students across India — current undergraduates, postgraduates, interns, and residents. It takes approximately five minutes.

A

Completely anonymous

No name, roll number, email, or institution identifier is collected. Your response cannot be traced back to you.

B

Five minutes

Short, structured questions. We respect your time — and you can stop at any point.

C

For research only

Responses are used solely to understand the scope of sexual violence in Indian medical education and advocate for change.

Before you begin — please read

This survey contains questions about sexual violence, harassment, and coercion. Some questions may be distressing. You are not obligated to answer every question, and you may stop at any point.

If anything in this survey causes you distress, please reach out: Tele-MANAS 14416, NCW 7827 170 170, or see all support resources →
